Discover Education City

A community unlike any other place in Qatar, Education City is home to branch campuses of some of the world’s leading educational institutes, sports and recreational facilities, numerous art installations, Qatar’s largest library, heritage sites, cultural institutions, and much more.

Places to Visit

During just a short walk or tram ride, you will discover the unique surroundings in Education City. Whether it is one of our many buildings designed by world-leading architects or inviting green spaces, there’s something for everyone.

Education City Universities

Our vibrant educational ecosystem includes seven international universities and our own Hamad Bin Khalifa University.

Carnegie Mellon University in Qatar

Since 2004, Carnegie Mellon University in Qatar has offered select programs in science, business, and technology that prepare graduates to be leaders, creators, and problem-solvers.

Georgetown University in Qatar

The liberal arts education at GU-Q offers students training in a wide range of subjects, along with the ability to specialize in key international affairs topics

Hamad Bin Khalifa University (HBKU)

Founded in 2010, Hamad Bin Khalifa University is a homegrown graduate-focused university and research institute that acts as a catalyst for transformative change in Qatar and the region.

Northwestern University in Qatar

To explore the world of media and communication, you can study at Northwestern University in Qatar (NU-Q), a modern media school that opened in Qatar in 2008.

Texas A&M University at Qatar

If you are interested in pursuing a career in engineering, Texas A&M University at Qatar (TAMUQ) offers one of the world’s premier engineering programs, built on its home campus’ international

Virginia Commonwealth University School of the Arts in Qatar

You can unleash your inner artist or designer by becoming part of VCUarts Qatar, a branch campus of Virginia Commonwealth University School of the Arts in Richmond, Virginia. The Gallery at VCUarts Qatar: Tour its spaces and discover where its threads of creativity lead you with its publishing platform.

Weill Cornell Medicine-Qatar

If you’re an aspiring doctor, you can join Weill Cornell Medicine-Qatar, which shares the tripartite mission of Weill Cornell Medicine in New York: a medical college dedicated to excellence in education, patient care, and research.

HEC Paris in Qatar

If you are a professional or an executive in a senior management position, you can enhance your leadership skills through the various programs offered at HEC Paris in Qatar.
